WHAT IS IT?

This event is a endurance championship with nine previously selected tracks and two races in each track (and month), in normal and reverse direction, totaling 18 races, and a points ranking that is filled with your points that you got each race (the points system is further down the page).

RULES

1 - The eight first tracks of the championship will last 15 minutes (more or less 30 laps), and the points will be distributed in each race

2 - The last track of the championship will have (in 2026) 60 laps (more or less 35-40 minutes), and the points will be distributed IN A SPECIAL POINT SYSTEM in each race

3 - AVOID HITTING PLAYERS WHO AREN'T ON THE SAME LAP AS YOU, DAMMIT
